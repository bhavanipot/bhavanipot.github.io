PlateCheck

PlateCheck, the winner of the Women in Engineering (WIE) hackathon, is a personalized nutritional app aimed at addressing nutrition gaps and promoting women’s overall health. The application allows users to upload a photo of their food, which is analyzed through APIs to identify its nutritional content and provide tailored recommendations.

Podcastify

Podcastify is a full-stack web application that transforms text input, such as textbooks (PDF/DOCX), into engaging audio podcasts using NLP and AI-driven summarization. Built with Python, Flask, Vue.js, and JavaScript. Includes chatbot functionality, summarization with spaCy/Heapq, and audio generation via Speechify.
